🏛️ Legibly – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Legibly reads form field labels and page context to generate explanations — never what you type into any field. Your personal answers, SSN, passwords, and sensitive information are never read, stored, or transmitted.

What Legibly Does

Legibly is a browser extension that helps people understand government form fields by providing plain-English explanations powered by AI. When you click on a form field, Legibly reads the field's label and surrounding page context to generate a helpful explanation.

What We Collect

Legibly collects the following information:

  • Account information — your email address when you create an account or sign in with Google, used solely for authentication and account management.
  • Form field labels — the text label of the field you click on (e.g. "Date of Birth", "Adjusted Gross Income"). This is the same text you can see on screen.
  • Page context — the page title, URL, and nearby heading or instruction text visible on the page, used to generate a more accurate explanation.
  • Usage count — the number of explanations you've generated in the current month, used to enforce free tier limits.

What We Never Collect

Legibly is designed to explicitly avoid collecting sensitive information:

  • We never read what you type into any form field
  • We never read your Social Security Number, tax ID, or government ID numbers
  • We never read your passwords, PINs, or security answers
  • We never read your financial account numbers, credit card numbers, or bank details
  • We never store your personal answers to any government form question

How Your Data Is Used

Field labels and page context are sent to our secure backend (hosted on Cloudflare Workers) which forwards them to the Anthropic API to generate a plain-English explanation. This data is used only to produce the explanation you requested and is not used for any other purpose.

Your email address is used to authenticate your account and associate your usage count with your account. It is also used to look up your subscription status with Stripe.

Third-Party Services

Legibly uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic — AI provider that processes field labels and context to generate explanations. Anthropic may log API requests per their own privacy policy at anthropic.com.
  • Supabase — handles user authentication and account management.
  • Stripe — processes payments for Legibly Pro subscriptions. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • Cloudflare Workers & KV — hosts our backend API and stores usage counts and subscription status.

Data is shared with these service providers only to the extent necessary to operate Legibly. We do not sell your data or share it with any third party for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

  • Usage counts reset automatically each month and are not retained long-term.
  • Subscription status is stored in Cloudflare KV and removed when your subscription ends.
  • Account data (email) is retained in Supabase until you delete your account.
  • Field labels and page context are not stored by Legibly after the explanation is generated. Anthropic's own data retention policies apply to API requests.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. You can sign out of Legibly at any time using the Sign Out button in the extension panel.

Children's Privacy

Legibly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated. Continued use of Legibly after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the extension store listing or at the email associated with your account.
